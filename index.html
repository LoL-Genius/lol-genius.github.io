What’s Missing Out There?

Many free websites offer stats, but are often just glorified match histories. If you want detailed analysis, you often have to pay for expensive 1-on-1 coaching.

LoL Genius bridges the gap between the two- offering detailed data and analytics to drive improvement!

Data & Analysis for Everyone

Even the top players need data. In conversations with pro analysts, they specifically highlighted a lack of data available beyond just vod reviews. Often teams have to hire a data analysts who’s full time job is to get game data.

LoL Genius is your own personal data analyst!

Detailed Data Across Games

Not only are basic stats like KDA, CS/min, and game time offered, LoL Genius can give detailed info like exp difference per lane at 10min or damage Even better, these stats are tracked across several games showing how you are doing.

If you want to know something, LoL Genius has an answer!
